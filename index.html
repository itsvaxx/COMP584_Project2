Sports Gallery

Basketball

Basketball is a team sport where two teams compete to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop. It requires agility, coordination, and strategic thinking.

Soccer

Soccer, known as football outside North America, is the world's most popular sport. Players use their feet to control and pass a ball toward the opponent's goal.

Tennis

Tennis is a racket sport played individually against a single opponent or between two teams of two players each. Matches are played on various court surfaces.

Swimming

Competitive swimming features various strokes including freestyle, breaststroke, backstroke, and butterfly. It's both an individual and team sport in the Olympics.

American Football

American football is a contact sport played by two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field. The objective is to advance an oval-shaped ball into the opponent's end zone.

Baseball

Baseball is a bat-and-ball game played between two opposing teams who take turns batting and fielding. The game proceeds when a player on the fielding team throws a ball.

Golf

Golf is a club-and-ball sport where players use various clubs to hit balls into a series of holes on a course in as few strokes as possible.

Track & Field

Track and field is a sport that includes athletic contests based on running, jumping, and throwing skills. The name is derived from the sport's typical venue: a stadium with an oval running track surrounding a grass field.

Winter Sports

Winter sports comprise sports played on snow or ice. Most are variations of skiing, ice skating, and sledding. Traditionally, such games were only played in cold areas during winter, but artificial snow and ice allow more flexibility. Popular winter sports include alpine skiing, cross-country skiing, snowboarding, ski jumping, speed skating, figure skating, bobsleigh, ice hockey, and curling.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport in which two teams of six players are separated by a net. Each team tries to score points by grounding a ball on the other team's court.

Rugby

Rugby is a contact team sport that originated in England in the first half of the 19th century. One of the two codes of rugby football, it is based on running with the ball in hand.

Badminton

Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. Although it may be played with larger teams, the most common forms of the game are singles and doubles.

Cycling

Cycling sport includes competitive physical activities using bicycles. It can take place on roads, tracks, or off-road terrain.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves, throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time in a boxing ring.

Gymnastics

Gymnastics is a sport that includes physical exercises requiring balance, strength, flexibility, agility, coordination, and endurance.

Martial Arts

Martial arts are codified systems and traditions of combat practiced for self-defense, competition, physical health, and entertainment.

Archery

Archery is the sport, practice, or skill of using a bow to shoot arrows. The word comes from the Latin arcus, meaning bow.

Weightlifting

Weightlifting is a sport in which athletes compete in lifting a barbell loaded with weight plates from the ground to overhead.
